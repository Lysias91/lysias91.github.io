What is Emotional Intelligence?

EQ or Emotional intelligence is defined as an individualâ€™s ability to perceive, control, evaluate, and express emotions.
EQ is centred on abilities like:

  • Self-awareness
  • Self-regulation
  • Motivation
  • Social-awareness
  • Social-regulation

This is different to a persons IQ, which is a number used to express the apparent relative intelligence of a person.
IQ represents abilities like::

  • Visual and spatial processing
  • Knowledge of the world
  • Fluid reasoning
  • Working memory and short-term memory
  • Quantitative reasoning
